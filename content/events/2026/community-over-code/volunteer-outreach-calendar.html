Community over Code Glasgow 2026 - October 11-14, Hilton Glasgow

Conference dates: October 11-14, 2026 - Glasgow, Scotland

This page tracks volunteer outreach tasks for Community over Code Glasgow - speaker support, local community engagement, attendee experience, and post-event follow-up. Each task needs a volunteer owner.

NOTE: Speaker outreach tasks require coordination through Brian Proffitt and the track chairs. Speaker contact information is not publicly shared.

Outreach resources:

  • Glasgow Schools & Universities
  • Glasgow Diversity & Inclusion Groups
  • Glasgow Tech Meetups

Glasgow guides (help us improve these!):

  • Bars & Pubs

Want to help promote the event? You don’t need to be assigned a task – just grab a ready-made post from our Social Media Promotion page and share it wherever you hang out online. Copy, paste, tweak, post!

Pick a task, click “Volunteer for this task!” to email the planning team, and we’ll get you set up.
