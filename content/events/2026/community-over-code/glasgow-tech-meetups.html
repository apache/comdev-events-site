Glasgow Tech Meetups - Outreach Contacts

Community over Code Glasgow 2026 - October 11-14, Hilton Glasgow

This page lists Glasgow-area tech meetups and developer communities. We’re inviting their members to attend Community over Code Glasgow (October 11-14, 2026) and participate in the hackathon.

Active Glasgow Tech Meetups

Python Glasgow

  • Website: https://www.meetup.com/python-glasgow/
  • Schedule: 1st Wednesday of every month
  • About: Monthly meetup for sharing perspectives and insights about Python. Hosts speakers from across the city.
  • Venue: The Gamer Club
  • Relevance: Python is central to many Apache projects. High overlap with potential hackathon participants.

Open Source Glasgow

  • Website: https://www.meetup.com/open-source-glasgow/
  • About: Open source focused meetup. Meets at The Gamer Club.
  • Relevance: DIRECTLY aligned - these are our people! High priority for outreach.

TechMeetup Glasgow

  • Website: https://www.meetup.com/techmeetup/
  • Schedule: Monthly
  • About: Monthly excuse for the tech community around Scotland to network, learn, and share new ideas. Informative presentations from peers, exciting projects, and networking.
  • Relevance: Broad tech audience, good for general awareness.

Glasgow Tech Social (The Glasgow Geeks)

  • Website: https://www.meetup.com/the-glasgow-geeks/
  • Schedule: First Friday of the month
  • About: Casual social meetup - beer and tech chat. Open to all levels.
  • Relevance: Informal channel to spread the word. Good for flyers or a quick pitch.

Scottish Technology Modernization (DevOps/SRE/IaC)

  • Website: https://www.meetup.com/glasgow-technology-devops-sre-iac-aiops-mlops/
  • About: Focused on DevOps, SRE, AIOps, IaC, MLOps. Modernizing business, operations, automation, next-gen development and scaling.
  • Relevance: Infrastructure and operations engineers who’d benefit from contributing to Apache infrastructure projects.

Cloud Native Glasgow

  • Website: https://www.meetup.com/cloudnativeglasgow/
  • About: Presentations and workshops on building, deploying, and maintaining software in cloud environments. Focus on containers, micro-services, and automation.
  • Relevance: Strong overlap with Apache projects (Kafka, Flink, Spark, etc.)

Ladies of Code (Glasgow)

  • Website: https://www.meetup.com/ladies-of-code-glasgow/
  • About: Supports women in tech with networking, knowledge sharing, and collaboration.
  • Relevance: Diversity outreach (also listed on diversity page).

How to Use This Page

When reaching out to meetup organizers:

  • Ask if you can give a 2-minute pitch at their next meetup before October
  • Offer to leave flyers or share a link in their Slack/Discord
  • Suggest a co-promotion: we mention their meetup at CoC, they mention CoC at their meetup
  • If they meet at The Gamer Club (several do), ask if there’s a community board
  • Emphasize the hackathon as a unique, in-person collaboration opportunity
