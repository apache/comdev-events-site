Call For Volunteers, Community over Code 2026

Community over Code is an event run by the community, for the community. That’s you!

We need your help to make it a success!

Help Promote the Event

The easiest way to help right now is to spread the word. We have ready-made social media posts you can copy, paste, and share on whatever platform you use:

  • Social Media Promotion page - Grab a post and share it! No signup required.

Volunteer Task Calendars

We have two calendars listing specific tasks that need an owner. Each task has a “Volunteer for this task!” link - just click it to sign up:

  • Volunteer Outreach Calendar - Speaker support, local outreach, attendee experience, post-event follow-up
  • Hackathon Outreach Calendar - Hackathon-specific publicity and coordination tasks

Volunteer Roles

Hackathon Coordination

Help organize and run the hackathon day - connecting projects with contributors and making sure everyone has what they need to be productive.

  • About hackathon coordination (general guide)
  • Glasgow hackathon page (this year’s event)
  • Glasgow hackathon outreach calendar (tasks to claim)

University & Educational Outreach

Connect with Glasgow’s colleges, universities, and coding bootcamps to bring students and educators into the Apache community.

  • About university outreach (general guide)
  • Glasgow schools & universities (local contacts and info)

Meetup & Community Outreach

Engage with local tech meetups and diversity-in-tech organizations to reach new audiences.

  • About meetup outreach (general guide)
  • Glasgow tech meetups (local groups to contact)
  • Glasgow diversity & inclusion groups (local organizations)

Speaker Support

Provide coaching and feedback to speakers - abstract reviews, slide feedback, and dress rehearsals.

  • About speaker support

Design

Create visual materials for outreach and on-site use - posters, flyers, social graphics, signage.

  • About design volunteering

Help Improve Our Glasgow Guides

Know Glasgow? Help us make these pages better for attendees:

  • Vegetarian & Vegan Restaurants - Add your favourite spots
  • Bars & Pubs - Recommend great places to socialize
  • Tourist Highlights - Add hidden gems and local tips

How to Get Involved

  • Join #community-over-code on the-asf.slack.com
  • Join apachecon.slack.com for day-to-day event coordination
  • Email planners@apachecon.com indicating your interest, and what you’d like to work on.

There are no specific requirements or qualifications for these jobs - just a passion for ASF community and a willingness to collaborate. We’re looking forward to working with you!
