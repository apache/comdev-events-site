Subject: Reminder: Submit your hackathon page by mid-September

Hi everyone,

Friendly reminder - if your project hasn't submitted its hackathon
page yet, now's the time! Participants are starting to browse the
list and choose which projects to hack on.

To add your page:
1. Create: source/events/2026/community-over-code/YOURPROJECT.md
 (template at: source/events/2026/community-over-code/sampleproject.md)
2. Link it from the table in hackathon.md
3. Submit a PR to: https://github.com/apache/comdev-events-site

Include:
- Your hackathon coordinator's name
- A task list (beginner, intermediate, advanced)
- Links to dev environment setup
- Who from your community is planning to attend

Questions? Reply here or ping me on Slack (#hackathon).

Thanks!
Rich
