Subject: 3 weeks to the hackathon — have you picked your project?

The Community over Code Glasgow hackathon is just 3 weeks away
(Oct 11-14)! 25+ Apache projects are ready with curated task lists
and mentors on hand.

If you haven't already:
1. Browse the projects: https://events.apache.org/events/2026/community-over-code/hackathon.html
2. Pick one (or two!) that interest you
3. Check out their "getting started" links
4. Show up ready to hack

No advance preparation required — but if you want a head start,
most projects list their dev environment setup instructions on their
hackathon page.

Register: https://communityovercode.org/registration/
Slack: #hackathon on http://s.apache.org/apachecon-slack
