Glasgow Schools & Universities - Outreach Contacts

Community over Code Glasgow 2026 - October 11-14, Hilton Glasgow

This page lists Glasgow-area educational institutions with computing/technology programmes. We’re inviting students and faculty to attend and participate in the hackathon at Community over Code Glasgow (October 11-14, 2026).

If you’re volunteering for student outreach, use this list as your starting point. Offer free or discounted student passes and highlight the hackathon as a hands-on learning opportunity.

Universities

University of Glasgow - School of Computing Science

  • Website: https://www.gla.ac.uk/schools/computing/
  • About: Ranked 6th in the UK for Computer Science (Times/Sunday Times 2025). Offers BSc, MSci, and postgraduate programmes. Strong in AI, algorithms, HCI, and software engineering.
  • Undergraduate admissions: https://www.gla.ac.uk/schools/computing/undergraduate/applicants/
  • Contact (general): https://www.gla.ac.uk/schools/computing/
  • Related group: Glasgow Women in Computing Science (GWiCS) - open to all staff and students: https://www.gla.ac.uk/schools/computing/diversity/activities/

University of Strathclyde - Department of Computer & Information Sciences

  • Website: https://www.strath.ac.uk/science/computerinformationsciences/
  • About: UK University of the Year (Daily Mail 2026), Scottish University of the Year (Sunday Times 2026). Research in HCI, information sciences, theoretical CS, and software systems.
  • UG admissions contact: cis-ugadmissions@strath.ac.uk
  • General enquiries: science-enquiries@strath.ac.uk
  • Contact page: https://www.strath.ac.uk/contactus/

Glasgow Caledonian University - School of Computing

  • Website: https://www.gcu.ac.uk/study/undergraduate/subjects/computing
  • About: Top-ranked modern university in the UK (Times/Sunday Times 2026). BCS-accredited computing programmes. Also runs “Women Do Cyber” programme in partnership with Cisco and SWiT.
  • Contact: https://www.gcu.ac.uk/
  • Cyber/diversity programme: https://www.gcu.ac.uk/aboutgcu/universitynews/gcu-looks-to-address-cyber-gender-gap

Coding Bootcamps & Training

CodeClan (Glasgow campus)

  • Website: https://codeclan.com/
  • About: Scotland’s leading digital skills academy. Offers 9-month Software Engineering (hybrid), 12-week Applied Agentic AI, and Data Engineering programmes. Project-based learning with cohort model.
  • Relevance: Alumni and current students are exactly the kind of people who’d benefit from a hackathon. They may also want to explore open source as a career pathway.

How to Use This Page

When you reach out to these institutions, consider:

  • Offering free or discounted student registration passes
  • Highlighting the hackathon as a portfolio-building opportunity
  • Mentioning that mentors will be available to help beginners
  • Suggesting faculty could bring a class as a field trip
  • Noting that the event is in central Glasgow (Hilton Glasgow, 1 William St)
