Meetup Outreach

Reaching out to local meetup groups is a great way to get a larger audience to our events, and serve the local technical community. As a meetuo outreach coordinator, you’ll be identifying those meetup groups, and getting the word out to them.

Volunteers should be local to the event venue.

What Meetup Outreach Involves

  • Identify relevant local meetup groups
  • Build relationships with local tech communities
  • Look for related topics, like legal, business, and communication, which may be interested in open source and Apache projects
  • Share information about Apache events with meetup communities
  • Encourage meetup participants to submit talks for the upcoming event

Types of Meetup Groups you might conenct with

We can engage with various types of groups:

  • Language-specific meetups (Java, Python, JavaScript, etc.)
  • Technology-focused groups (cloud, data, web development)
  • Open source and community groups
  • General developer meetups

Get Involved

If you’re active in local meetup communities or have experience organizing meetups, your help would be valuable. Join us on the apachecon.slack.com Slack instance to volunteer.

Contact the Event Planning mailing list to discuss meetup outreach opportunities.
