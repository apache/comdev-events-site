Design Volunteers

Visual design plays a crucial role in promoting Apache events and reaching diverse audiences. Design volunteers create materials that help communicate the value and accessibility of Apache events.

Design Opportunities

Posters and Print Materials

  • Event posters and landing pages for different outreach audiences
  • Flyers for university and meetup distribution

Design Considerations

  • Maintain consistency with Apache brand guidelines
  • Create inclusive, welcoming visuals
  • Design for accessibility
  • Consider diverse audiences and contexts
  • Support multiple formats and sizes

Skills Needed

  • Graphic design experience
  • Understanding of web design principles
  • Attention to accessibility standards

Get Involved

If you have design skills and want to help promote Apache events, please come talk to us! Subscrie to the Event planning mailing list and join us in Slack to discuss design volunteer opportunities.
