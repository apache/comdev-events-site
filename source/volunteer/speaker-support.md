---
title: Speaker Support and Coaching
---

Great presentations help spread knowledge about Apache projects and the ASF. Our speaker support volunteers provide coaching and feedback to help speakers deliver engaging, effective talks.

## What speaker support volunteers do

As a volunteer, you'll offer several types of speaker support:

<img src="/images/speaker.jpg" alt="Speaker" class="pull-right">

### CFP and Abstract Support

* Review and provide feedback on conference proposals
* Help refine talk titles and abstracts
* Advise on targeting proposals to specific audiences
* Suggest improvements to increase acceptance chances

### Slide Review and Editing

* Review presentation slides for clarity and flow
* Provide feedback on visual design and readability
* Suggest improvements to technical content
* Help ensure slides align with talk objectives and ASF branding

### Presentation Rehearsal

* Conduct dress rehearsal sessions via video chat
* Provide constructive feedback on delivery
* Help with timing and pacing
* Offer tips on handling questions
* Build speaker confidence

## Who Can Help

We welcome volunteers with:

* Speaking experience at technical conferences
* Presentation design skills
* Technical writing background
* Teaching or training experience

## How to volunteer

Join the **#abstracts** channel on the [apachecon.slack.com](http://s.apache.org/apachecon-slack) Slack instance to connect with speakers and other coaches.

Contact the [Event Planning mailing list](mailto:planners@apachecon.com) if you're interested in providing speaker support.
